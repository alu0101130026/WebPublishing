FC Hradec Králové - Teplice

The footballers of Hradec Králové defeated Teplice 3: 0 in the 12th round of the first league and moved up to sixth place in the table. Pavel Dvořák scored two goals for the rookie of the highest competition, Jakub Rada added one. After the third loss, Teplice remains penultimate and their new coach Jiří Jarošík is still waiting in vain for the first point.
